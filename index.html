How to Order

Step 1: Browse and Select Your Device

Use our web app to explore the available iPhone models, colors, and storage options. Once the desired device is found, click on it to confirm its details.

Step 2: Contact Us on WhatsApp

After choosing the device, click the " Confirm Buy" button. Our team will assist in confirming your selected model, color, and storage availability, and guide you through the next steps of the process.

Step 3: Make Your Payment

We offer two payment options: full payment for expedited processing or a flexible lay-by plan. Choose the option that suits your needs, and our team will provide the necessary payment details.

Step 4: Arrange Delivery

Upon payment confirmation, please provide delivery details. For local deliveries, we can arrange in-person drop-offs. For other areas, we will coordinate delivery through our trusted courier partners, typically within 3-7 business days.
